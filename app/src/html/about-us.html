About Organ Donation Platform

A blockchain-powered decentralized application that brings transparency, trust, and efficiency to the organ donation ecosystem.

Why Blockchain?

Traditional organ donation systems suffer from lack of transparency, manual verification, and delays. Blockchain eliminates these issues by providing immutable records and decentralized trust.

Secure & Transparent

Smart contracts ensure donor and recipient data remains tamper-proof, auditable, and securely handled without centralized authority.
